Who We Are

About WanderLux

We're a small team of travel consultants who believe holidays should be planned around the traveller, not a brochure.

Our Mission

Travel planning that actually listens

WanderLux Travel Agency was founded on a simple frustration: most travel sites sell the same five packages to everyone. We built WanderLux to do the opposite — every itinerary starts with a conversation about how you like to travel, not a list of what's on special.

From first enquiry to the day you land back home, our consultants stay attached to your trip. That means real people checking pricing, real recommendations based on what you've told us, and real support if your flight gets delayed at 2am.

Guided tour briefing, Swiss Alps

Customer Service Values

What guides every booking

  • Transparent pricing — no hidden fees, ever. What you see in your cost estimate is what you're quoted.
  • Honest recommendations — we'll tell you if a destination doesn't suit your dates, budget, or travel style.
  • Always reachable — a named consultant and 24/7 emergency support for every booking.
  • Accessible by design — our website and our service are built to work for everyone, including screen reader and keyboard users.
