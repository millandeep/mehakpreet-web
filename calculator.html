How we calculate your estimate

Your total is built from three parts: a daily travel cost per traveller (transport, meals, and activities), an accommodation cost per night for the group, and a flat booking and planning fee. Your chosen travel style then scales travel and accommodation costs - Budget trims them by 25%, Standard keeps them as listed, and Luxury increases them by 50% for upgraded stays and experiences. These are illustrative preset rates for demonstration purposes, not live pricing.
